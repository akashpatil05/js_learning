Our History

2020: TravelBloom was founded with a simple vision - to help people find their perfect destination.

2021: Launched our first mobile app and expanded our database to 50+ destinations.

2022: Reached 100,000 active users and established partnerships with major travel agencies.

2023: Introduced AI-powered recommendations and expanded to 150+ destinations worldwide.

2024: Became the leading travel recommendation platform with 500,000+ users globally.
